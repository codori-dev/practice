Meet various games

Traditional Korean games have a long history, passed down through generations as a way to celebrate community and joy. They were often played during holidays like Lunar New Year or Chuseok, when families and neighbors gathered together. One of the most iconic games is Yutnori, a board game played with four wooden sticks that determine how far the pieces move.
